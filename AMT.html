UCI Accounts Management Tool

The My UCI Account Management Tool (AMT) is a platform that will allow UCI students, employees and affiliates to manage and view their identity data.

Problems

Currently as a member of the University, updating identify information about yourself requires navigating to multiple websites to manage your UCInetID, enroll in MFA, update your UCI Directory data, change your mail routing, managing emergency notification settings and etc. Each site has its own look and feel and its own user interface, which can lead to confusion for a user.




Solutions

AMT plans to simplify this by providing a single platform for a user to manage all their identity data. By building this platform OIT can also include planned feature updates such as viewing the last sites you signed into, your data privacy release and etc.
