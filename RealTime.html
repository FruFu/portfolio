UCI Real-Time Ops Web (Redesign)

A web that has been use by all the managers in the UCI Student Center & Event Services to automate and show the Student Center shift report data.

Understand Current Problem

Information Overload

Lots of Unused Space

Zero Flexibility (No filter, No user control, No search option)

Research

User Interview

Conducted over 10 interviews with student employees and operation managers. My questions mainly focused on what data are the most important for them, how often they view this data, and any feedback on the current UI design.

Solution

Nielsen's ten heuristic evaluation

After conducting all the interviews, I decided to use nielsen's ten heuristic evaluation to help me identify the usability problems and make improvement on the redesign.

I created the wireframes for couple of the main pages to which allows me to present my ideas to the managers.
