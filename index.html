About Me

I am a graduated student at UC Irvine, focusing on Informatics (Human–computer interaction). I am looking to start a career in the UI/UX field. I have a passion for solving problems and love learning how people think.

My Philosophy:
Good design begins with a human story. Designs that are instinctive and intuitive, that show restraint, and are nearly invisible will accomplish great things. As the demand for media and gadgets continues to rise, so will the need for responsible design that humanizes technology in ways that stand out from increased competition.

My Process:
Translating business concepts and requirements into an effective design begins with understanding user roles to generate User Flows and lo-fidelity sketches or wireframes. User Personas are generated in collaboration with executive sponsors, stakeholders, the project manager, brand managers, developers, and other team members for usability testing. Then as the project progresses and with executive approval, hi-fidelity mockups and prototypes are created for testing user reactions and interactions.
