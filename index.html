About Me

“People ignore design that ignores people”

— Frank Chimero

My philosophy is good design begins with a human story. Designs that are instinctive and intuitive, that show restraint, and are nearly invisible will accomplish great things. As the demand for media and gadgets continues to rise, so will the need for responsible design that humanizes technology in ways that stand out from increased competition.

I graduated from UCI with a degree in Informatics (Human–computer interaction) and worked as an IT Technician for 2 years on campus until I transfer over as a UI/UX designer for my remaining college years. With my academic experience and tech-savvy skill sets, I want to create user-driven experiences and engaging design campaigns that make lasting impressions on people.

Feel free to contact me: edwardf1@uci.edu
